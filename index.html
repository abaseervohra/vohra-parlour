About

VOHRA BEAUTY PARLOUR

Vohra Beauty Parlour was launched in 2016 by Erum Vohra as a small beauty salon operated from her home in Karachi. Today, as we mark the 8th year of our operations in Pakistan, we stand tall as one of the emerging brands in the beauty industry of Pakistan.

Our journey from a single branch primarily offering bridal makeup services to a rapidly growing network of modern and full-service Beauty and Hair salons across Pakistan is marked by our commitment to delivering experiences that not only move hearts and minds but also fuel a passion for progress and growth
